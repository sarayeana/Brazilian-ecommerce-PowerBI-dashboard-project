# Business Understanding

## Project Title

Brazilian E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Brazilian Olist E-Commerce dataset using Microsoft Power BI.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard that enables decision-makers to monitor sales performance, customer behavior, seller performance, product trends, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

The dashboard is designed using a Star Schema data model, DAX measures, and interactive visualizations following business intelligence best practices.

---

# Business Problem

An e-commerce company generates thousands of orders across multiple product categories, sellers, and customer locations.

Management needs a centralized dashboard to answer critical business questions such as:

- How much revenue is being generated?
- Which products and categories perform best?
- Which sellers contribute the most revenue?
- How do customers behave over time?
- Are deliveries completed on time?
- How do delivery performance and customer reviews relate?

Without a centralized dashboard, answering these questions requires manually querying multiple tables, making decision-making slow and inefficient.

---

# Project Goal

Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# Stakeholders

The dashboard is designed for multiple business users:

- Executive Management
- Sales Managers
- Marketing Team
- Operations Team
- Product Managers

Each stakeholder can use the dashboard to explore KPIs, trends, and operational performance.

---

# Business Objectives

The dashboard should help stakeholders:

- Monitor sales performance.
- Track revenue growth over time.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Evaluate seller performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze delivery efficiency.
- Monitor customer satisfaction through review scores.
