
# 📊 Dashboard Guide

## Overview

This project contains five interactive dashboard pages designed to provide a comprehensive view of business performance using the Brazilian E-Commerce (Olist) dataset.

Each dashboard is built for a specific business audience and focuses on answering key business questions through interactive visualizations, KPIs, drill-through pages, tooltips, and synchronized slicers.

---

# Dashboard Navigation

1. Executive Overview
2. Sales Analysis
3. Customer Analysis
4. Product & Seller Analysis
5. Delivery & Customer Review Analysis

All pages share synchronized slicers to provide a consistent user experience.

---

# Common Features

Every dashboard page includes:

- Interactive slicers
- Cross-filtering between visuals
- Dynamic titles
- Report tooltips
- Consistent color theme
- Responsive layout
- Drill-through functionality (where applicable)

# Dashboard 1 — Executive Overview

## Purpose

The Executive Overview dashboard provides management with a high-level summary of business performance.

It is designed for executives who need to understand the company's overall performance within a few seconds.

---

## Target Audience

- CEO
- Business Managers
- Executives
- Stakeholders

---

## KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value
- Average Review Score

---

## Visuals

### Revenue Trend

**Visual:** Line Chart

Shows how revenue changes over time.

Business Question:

> Is revenue growing consistently?

---

### Revenue by Product Category

**Visual:** Clustered Bar Chart

Compares revenue generated by each product category.

Business Question:

> Which categories contribute the most revenue?

---

### Revenue by Customer State

**Visual:** Bar Chart

Displays sales distribution across Brazilian states.

Business Question:

> Which states generate the highest revenue?

---

### Top 10 Products

**Visual:** Column Chart

Displays the highest revenue-generating products.

Business Question:

> Which products are driving sales?

---

### Monthly Orders

**Visual:** Column Chart

Shows order volume by month.

Business Question:

> How has purchasing activity changed over time?

---

## Available Slicers

- Order Year
- Customer State
- Product Category
- Seller State
